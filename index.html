About us


Mahadevan (Dev) is a seasoned software engineer with over 20 years of experience building high-impact technology solutions across diverse industries. He has worked with organizations of all sizes — from startups to multinational corporations — delivering scalable, secure, and performance-driven applications.

His expertise spans software development methodologies, backend and application engineering, system architecture, and technical leadership. Dev has consistently played a key role in designing, leading, and shipping mission-critical software projects that solve real business challenges.

Alongside his engineering work, Dev is also a recognized Udemy instructor, known for making complex programming topics simple and enjoyable for learners worldwide. His teaching focuses on real-world application, helping developers grow practical confidence in software development

Throughout his career, Dev has successfully guided teams, mentored junior engineers, and shaped technical roadmaps that align engineering goals with business impact.

He holds an MS in Computer Science from New York and continues to explore and share the possibilities of modern software development.

Whether building products, teaching developers, or collaborating on innovative ideas, Dev’s mission remains constant — to use technology as a tool to improve lives and inspire curiosity.
